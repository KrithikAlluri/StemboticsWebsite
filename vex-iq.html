Compete in International Competitions with VEX IQ

At StemBotics, we're excited to introduce your child to VEX IQ, a robotics platform designed to inspire the next generation of innovators.

VEX IQ provides hands-on learning experiences that develop essential skills like critical thinking, problem-solving, and collaboration in a fun and engaging way.

By joining VEX IQ at StemBotics, your child will have the opportunity to:

  • Build and program robots using the VEX IQ system
  • Participate in exciting competitions and challenges
  • Work in teams to solve real-world problems
  • Gain valuable STEM skills for future success

Give your child a head start in STEM education with VEX IQ at StemBotics!

When you register, you'll be enrolled in our monthly subscription program, which includes access to all our classes for just $100 per month.
