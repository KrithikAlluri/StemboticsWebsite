Policies and Terms

  • Refund and Dispute Policy: At Stembotics Academy, we strive to ensure customer satisfaction with our STEM education programs. If you are not completely satisfied with your enrollment in a course or workshop, you may request a refund within 30 days of the program start date. Refunds will be issued minus any applicable administrative fees.
  • Cancellation Policy: If you need to cancel your enrollment in a course or workshop at Stembotics Academy, please notify us at least 14 days prior to the program start date to receive a full refund. Cancellations made less than 14 days before the start date may be subject to a cancellation fee.
  • Transaction Currency: All transactions at Stembotics Academy are conducted in United States Dollars (USD). We accept payments in USD for course enrollments, workshop registrations, and any other products or services offered by our learning center.
  • Security Capabilities and Policy for Transmission of Payment Card Details: Stembotics Academy takes the security of your payment card details seriously. We utilize industry-standard encryption and secure payment processing technologies to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of your sensitive information during transmission. Your payment card details are encrypted and transmitted securely to prevent unauthorized access or interception.
  • Consumer Data Privacy Policy: Protecting the privacy of our customers' personal information is a top priority at Stembotics Academy. We collect and process personal data, including names, contact information, and educational background, solely for the purpose of providing our STEM education services. We adhere to strict data privacy laws and regulations and do not share or sell customer data to third parties without consent. Our privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information. By enrolling in our programs or using our services, you agree to abide by these policies. If you have any questions or concerns about our policies, please contact us for assistance.
